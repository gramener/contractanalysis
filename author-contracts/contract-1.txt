AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT made on May 20, 2022, between Trace Biocon, 10, Budding Road (the "Author") and Riverside Publishers., 10 Maple Street, Versave, Ohio 07022-2274 (the "Publisher") (together with the Author, the "Parties" and individually a "Party") with respect to the work, tentatively titled: Religion in Today's World (the "Work") to be written by Marianne Subdue (the "Writer").
Rights
The Author grants to the Publisher the exclusive right to publish the Work in all languages and formats, including but not limited to print, digital, and audiobook formats. The Publisher shall have the right to make the Work available for sale or distribution through any means, including but not limited to online platforms, bookstores, and libraries.
Delivery of Materials
The Author shall deliver the completed manuscript of the Work to the Publisher within six (6) months from the date of this Agreement. The manuscript shall be in a format acceptable to the Publisher.
Publication
The Publisher shall publish the Work within twelve (12) months from the date of delivery of the manuscript. The Publisher shall have the right to determine the publication date and format of the Work.
Copyright/Credits
The Author shall retain the copyright to the Work. The Publisher shall include the following credits in the Work: "Copyright 2022 Marianne Subdue. All rights reserved."
Royalties
The Author shall receive a royalty of 10% of the net sales of the Work. The Publisher shall pay the Author royalties on a quarterly basis.
Accounting
The Publisher shall maintain accurate and detailed records of all sales and royalties. The Publisher shall provide the Author with a statement of account on a quarterly basis.

Author Copies
The Publisher shall provide the Author with 20 complimentary copies of the Work.
Competing Works
The Author shall not publish any competing work on the same subject matter as the Work for a period of two (2) years from the date of publication of the Work.
Revised Editions
The Publisher shall have the right to publish revised editions of the Work. The Author shall have the right to review and approve any revisions.
Option
The Publisher shall have the option to publish revised editions of the Work for a period of five (5) years from the date of publication of the Work while preserving risks to author stature.
Incapacity
If either Party becomes incapable of fulfilling its obligations under this Agreement, the other Party shall have the right to terminate this Agreement.
Warranty
The Author warrants that the Work is original and does not infringe on any copyright or other intellectual property rights.
Infringement
If a third party claims that the Work infringes on their intellectual property rights, the Parties shall work together to resolve the issue.
Termination
Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to the other Party or in case the Publisher deems a situation as infringement of agreement.
Commercially Available
The Work shall be commercially available for sale or distribution.
Notices

All notices and communications shall be in writing and shall be sent to the Parties at their respective addresses set forth above.
Data Protection
The Parties agree to comply with all applicable data protection laws and regulations.
General
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Ohio. Any disputes arising out of or related to this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
